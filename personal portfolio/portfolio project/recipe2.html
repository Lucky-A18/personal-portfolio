Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil

Instructions

  1. Slice chicken and cook in sesame oil.
  2. Add sliced peppers and onions.
  3. Add soy sauce and stir-fry till done.
