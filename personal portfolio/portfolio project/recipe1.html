üçù Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 eggs
  • 50g Parmesan cheese
  • Black pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Fry the pancetta until crispy.
  3. Whisk the eggs with Parmesan and black pepper.
  4. Mix everything while pasta is warm to create a creamy sauce.
